spoiled for choice

spare time can be stressful, and i don't think that's usually the expectation. but, if your spare time is fleeting, and after so much activity and work you're allowed to relax, how do you relax.... with efficiency? maybe there's something wrong in the way we think about our time.

college especially has thrown this issue of 'productive' relaxation in my face, and i recently watched a video essay that sums it up fairly well. no, it's not a new thing to fall into an anti-capitalist ideological hole as a college student, but there are still valuable life lessons that seems to be missing from most media forms these days.
